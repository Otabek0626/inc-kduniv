Introduction

KDU Global International Networking Camp (INC) is an annual event organized and fully funded by Kyungdong University Global Campus to provide international students with a unique opportunity to discover Korean culture, build networks, and complete skills-oriented technical and training courses

INC 2023 is composed of two sessions. Session I will last from July 10 to August 11 and is designed to expose program participants to contemporary vogues of South Korea through professional classes on K-Beauty, K-Pop, K-Cuisine, and K-Sports. In Session II students will have a chance to enroll to undergraduate degree courses at KDU Global Campus and study in a culturally diverse, truly global environment for a semester from August 28 to December 15, 2023. Both sessions offer various cultural activities, language classes, and exciting field trips throughout Korea!

Selected program participants will be granted full scholarships that cover tuition fees, on-campus accommodation, and organizational costs of field trips and professional classes.

We welcome and look forward to having you in KDU Global INC 2023!
